Publications

Vincent A. Cicirello has published over 40 refereed journal and conference papers on topics including artificial intelligence, genetic algorithms, evolutionary computation, swarm intelligence, machine learning, multi-agent systems, computer-aided engineering, computer-aided design, and computer science education.
