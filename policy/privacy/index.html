Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2018.

Data We Collect and Why: We do not collect any personally identifiable information. We may collect information about your computer, including your operating system (e.g., Windows, Linux, etc.), web browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Edge, etc.), and browser type (e.g., desktop, phone, tablet), for system administration and in order to create reports to help improve the performance of our website. Additionally, we may collect information about which pages you visit within our website. This too is for the purpose of maintaining a high quality experience for visitors. This is statistical data about our users' browsing actions and patterns, and does not identify any individual.

Cookies: The only cookies in use on our site are for Google Analytics with Anonymized IP and Session Only. Google Analytics is a web analytics tool that helps website owners understand how visitors engage with their website. Google Analytics customers can view a variety of reports about how visitors interact with their website so that they can improve it. Like many services, Google Analytics uses first-party cookies to track visitor interactions as in our case, where they are used to collect information about how visitors use our site. We then use the information to compile reports and to help us improve our site.

Session Only Cookies: We specifically use Session Only cookies, by setting the cookie expiration to 0 seconds, which should cause your web browser to delete the cookie upon closing your web browser. In this way, each time you visit our website, you're viewed as a different anonymous user. We do not track your usage of our website across visits.

Anonymized IP: Google Analytics collects information anonymously. We specifically use the Anonymized IP setting, which directs Google Analytics to anonymize IP addresses. The actual IP addresses of visitors are not written to storage. Google Analytics reports website trends without identifying individual visitors. For more information on how Google may use data collected via Google Analytics, see Google's Privacy Policy https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/.

Site Search: The Site Search feature of our website utilizes a static index of the pages of our website. All queries are processed locally, via javascript, within your web browser (and not on our web server). To optimize performance of our Site Search, we use a feature of HTML5, called Local Storage to cache a copy of the static index locally within your web browser upon first usage of the Site Search feature. We do not transmit any data from this local index of our website back to our site.
