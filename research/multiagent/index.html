Multi-Agent Systems / Multi-Robot Systems

Although much of my research on multiagent / multirobot systems is specifically related to swarm intelligence, I have additionally conducted research related to other aspects of these systems, including market-based coordination, security engineering of multiagent systems, issues related to mobile agents on ad hoc wireless networks, power aware agents, and multiagent collaboration tools.
