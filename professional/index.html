Professional Activities

I regularly contribute to the professional community in a variety of ways, including serving on editorial boards, as well as on conference and professional society committees. This page includes a sampling of some of my professional activities, loosely organized by professional society.

Consortium for Computing Sciences in Colleges (CCSC)

  • Eastern Region Steering Committee: I have been a member of the Steering Committee for the CCSC Eastern Region since 2012. The Steering Committee provides guidance and oversight for eastern regional activities, including the annual regional conference.
  • Conference Chair for the 2015 eastern regional conference, hosted at Stockton University.
  • Conference Chair for the 2012 eastern regional conference, which would have been held at Stockton University, but was canceled at the last minute due to Hurricane Sandy. The conference proceedings was still published, and most conference presentations were rescheduled as part of the following year's conference.
  • Workshops, Tutorials, Panels Chair for the annual eastern regional conference in 2018 and 2017.
  • Speakers Chair for the annual eastern regional conference in 2016.
  • Poster Chair for the annual eastern regional conference in 2014.
  • I have also frequently served as a paper reviewer for the annual eastern regional conference, as well as occasionally for the northeastern regional conference, and have often served as a poster judge or as a session chair at the eastern regional conference.

European Alliance for Innovation (EAI)

  • Editor in Chief, Cognitive Communications: I am currently serving (since May 2020) as one of two Co Editors in Chief of Cognitive Communications, an open access journal published by the EAI. Like all of EAI's journals, Cognitive Communications is refereed with a blind review process, and is truly open access with no author fees and no subscription fees.
  • Area Editor, Scalable Information Systems: I am currently serving (since March 2020) as an Area Editor for Scalable Information Systems, an open access journal published by the EAI. Like all of EAI's journals, Scalable Information Systems is refereed with a blind review process, and is truly open access with no author fees and no subscription fees.
  • Special Tracks Chair and a Program Committee Vice Chair for the 9th EAI International Conference on Bio-inspired Information and Communications Technologies in 2015.
  • Track Co-Chair for the EAI BICT 2015 Special Track on Combinatorial Optimization.
  • Program Committee member for the 11th EAI International Conference on Bio-inspired Information and Communications Technologies in 2019.
  • Program Committee member for the 10th EAI International Conference on Bio-inspired Information and Communications Technologies in 2017.
  • Occasionally serve as a reviewer for some of EAI's other open access journals, including Cloud Systems, Energy Web, and Pervasive Health and Technology.

Florida Artificial Intelligence Research Society (FLAIRS)

The annual International FLAIRS conference is one of my favorite conferences to attend. It is always well organized, with high quality key notes, as well as high quality paper sessions. I have been serving as a member of the Program Committee every year since 2011, including FLAIRS-24, FLAIRS-25, FLAIRS-26, FLAIRS-27, FLAIRS-28, FLAIRS-29, FLAIRS-30, FLAIRS-31, FLAIRS-32, and FLAIRS-33.
